FaerieLand Rescue Inc. is an all-volunteer nonprofit organization run solely on adoption fees and donations. The rescue serves German Shepherds & Welsh Corgis in need. Our network of devoted foster families provides a safe haven to our rescues as they make their way to quality, loving homes of their own. All adoption fees are used to cover spay/neuter procedures, shots, worming, microchips, emergency boarding, food, medications, and other surgeries as needed for adoptable dogs.

Your donation will go directly to providing care for a German Shepherd or Corgi in need. A donation of just $10 will cover one night of emergency boarding. $25 can provide food and supplies for one foster dog for two weeks. And there are many additional ways your generous donation can help:

  • $15 - Pays for one microchip
  • $25 - A trip to the groomer to help a battered rescue dog show its best
             side to potential adopters
  • $100 - Vaccines to prevent rabies, distemper, and parvo for one dog
  • $200 - Nutritious dog food to feed 50 dogs for 2 weeks
  • $500 - Spay or neuter surgeries for four dogs
